Neymar Jr

At the age of five,he started playing football for Grandoli,a club coached by his father. In 1995,Messi switched to Newell's Old Boys. At the age of 11,he was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Ronaldo joined Clube Desportivo Nacional of Madeira and then transferred to Sporting Clube de Portugal,where he played for that club's various youth football teams before making his debut on Sporting's first team in 2002.

Paulo Dybala

He is an Argentine footballer who plays as a forward for Italian club Juventus and the Argentina national team.He began his career as a professional footballer in 2011 in the Argentine Second division.

Mesut Ozil

Mesut Ozil started his senior career as a member of his hometown club Schalke 04 in the Bundesliga.At the age of 22 due to his performance,he garnered everyone’s attention at the 2010 FIFA World Cup.

Mauro Icardi

Icardi started his football career by playing for local side Vecindario in Gran Canaria and scored over 500 goals in their youth categories. He was with Barcelona from 2008 to 2011.

Di Maria

Di María was born on 14 February 1988 in Rosario, Santa Fe, as one of three children of Miguel and Diana, and grew up in Perdriel. As an infant, he was unusually active, and on recommendation of a doctor was signed up for football at age three.

Kylian Mbappé

Mbappé was born in Paris.His father, Wilfried, is from Cameroon, and, as well as being Mbappé's agent, is a football coach, while his mother, Fayza Lamari, is of Algerian origin and is a former handball player.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah was born on June 15,1992 to average middle class parents.At first,Salah played football just for fun, but he started taking it as a serious career opportunity when he joined a local youth club'El Mokawloon'in Cairo.

Harry Kane

Born and raised in the London borough of Waltham Forest, Kane began his career at Tottenham Hotspur, where, after fast progression through the team's youth academy, he was promoted to the senior team in 2009, at age 16.

Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin played in the 2014 FIFA World Cup and the UEFA Euro 2016.Kevin spent early few years of his life travelling through England and Africa,due to his mother's background.

Philippe Coutinho

Coutinho began his youth career with Vasco da Gama and played for them between 1999-2008 till he made his senior team debut for Inter Milan.After loan spells at Espanyol and Vasco de Gama,the Brazilian moved to Liverpool in 2013.
